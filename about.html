About Powered PYQs

Powered PYQs is a study tool for Class 10 board exam preparation. It organizes previous year questions, solutions, saved questions, and recommended resources so students can revise more efficiently.

What You Can Do

Browse questions by subject and chapter, filter by year, save important questions, and review step-by-step solutions.
